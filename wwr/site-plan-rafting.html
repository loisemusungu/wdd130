Overview

Purpose

Establishing an online presence aims to significantly increase visibility and reach, enabling potential customers to easily find and book our services. It serves to provide essential trip details, safety information, and an online booking system, enhancing convenience and customer experience. Additionally, an online platform helps build our brand reputation through testimonials, social media integration, and engaging content.

Audience

Our target customers are adventure enthusiasts, families, and corporate groups aged 18 to 50 with moderate to high disposable incomes, seeking thrilling yet safe rafting experiences. They expect detailed trip information, including difficulty levels, costs, safety measures, and booking options, alongside testimonials and reviews. Their unmet needs include easy access to reliable trip details, user-friendly booking systems, and reassurance regarding safety and quality. Primarily accessing our site via mobile devices, they also use desktops and laptops for comprehensive planning and reservations, necessitating a responsive and mobile-friendly website.
